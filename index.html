Hello

My life journey has led me to the exciting world of coding, developing websites, and creating digital tools. My background is in photography and international development and I am excited to combine these skills to be a part in making the world a better place. I am based out of beautiful Bloomington Indiana, but love traveling around the world, especially Southeast Asia!

About Me

After receiving a degree in philosophy, my desire was to play music, make friends, and explore the world. These are wonderful aspirations, but I eventually learned that I also needed tangible skills to solve problems big and small. I love web development because people from all over the world can unite together. Whether it is creating tools and interfaces for local business, or organizing data for a non profit, location does not hinder ones ability to engage.


I can't think of anything that blends so well with other interests. There is a software component to everything, and with the drive to improve and integrate, our world will keep changing and improving.


While I am only a Jr level developer, I have the desire to work with a team and advance my skills. My background in digital imagery is a complimentary skill that can be an asset to your team and organization.
